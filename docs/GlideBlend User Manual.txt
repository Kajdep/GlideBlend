GlideBlend User Manual
=====================

Purpose
-------
GlideBlend merges two clips at the most visually similar frames so the handoff feels cleaner than a hard cut.

Quick Start
-----------
1. Launch GlideBlend.
2. Load Clip 1 and Clip 2.
3. Click GlideBlend.
4. Review the merged preview.
5. Click Save MP4 and choose an output location.

Best Results
------------
- Use clips with the same resolution, frame rate, and codec when possible.
- Keep similar lighting and composition across the transition.
- For AI video workflows, stitch short generated shots in sequence rather than trying to blend unrelated scenes.

Notes
-----
- All processing happens locally on your machine.
- No footage is uploaded by the app.
- Large source files may take longer to analyze and save.
